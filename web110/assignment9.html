Activity 1: What is Accesibiliy? Accessibility means designing websites so everyone, no matter what disability or impairment they have. So when creating a website, it involves considering various impairments—such as visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive challenges—and ensuring that web content is still accessible them. For example, adding text descriptions to images helps visually impaired users understand the content, while providing captions for videos aids those who are deaf or hard of hearing. By focusing on accessibility for my future work, I can create a more inclusive and user-friendly web experience for everyone.
